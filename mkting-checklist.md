# Marketing Event Checklist

## Pre-Event Checklist

* [ ] **Event Recommendation:** Highlight the business reasons, target account presence, and expected outcomes.
* [ ] **Event ROI Completed:** Complete all ROI requirements in event-planner tool.
* [ ] **Approval:** Secure event approval.
* [ ] **Event Planning:** Create detailed event plan
* [ ] **Campaign Planning:** Finalize the pre-event strategy, campaign plan, and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).
* [ ] **Campaign Activation:** Initiate pre-event campaign
* [ ] **Campaign Evaluation:** Optimize the active campaign, update the CRM, and track scheduled meetings.
* [ ] **Marketing Tracker:** Log the initial campaign details and expected results into the 2026 marketing tracker.

---

## During-Event Checklist

* [ ] **Live Coverage:** Execute social media coverage and capture live customer stories and content.
* [ ] **Lead Management:** Capture, score, categorize, and properly hand over leads (including booked meetings and executive requests).

---

## Post-Event Checklist

* [ ] **Event Data Capture:** All internal attendees, external contacts and companies, and meets are logged in event-planner tool
* [ ] **Event Wrap-up:** Close out vendors, reconcile the budget, gather feedback, send thank-you emails, finalize post-event content and campaigns.
* [ ] **Content & Amplification:** Consolidate event content and amplify coverage (press releases, case studies, interviews).
* [ ] **CRM Update:** Upload all final lead data to the CRM.
* [ ] **Reporting & Activations:** Complete final event reporting in event-planner tool.
* [ ] **Event Completed:** Event status is moved to "OCCURRED".
