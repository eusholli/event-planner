# Event Quick Start Guide

Welcome to the Event Planner! Follow these simplified steps to get your event set up, configured for ROI tracking, and ready for management.

---

## 1. Add a New Event

*Access Level Required: Root or Marketing*

1. **Navigate to Events**: Go to the **Events Portfolio** from the main navigation.
2. **Create Event**: Click the **"+"** (New Event) button.
3. **Basic Details**: Enter the required event data:
    - **Event Name**: e.g., *Cloud Innovators Summit 2026*
    - **Slug**: A unique URL identifier (e.g., `cloud-summit-26`).
    - **Dates & Location**: Start/End dates, Region, Timezone, and Address.
4. **AI Scraper (Optional)**: Paste the event's official URL and click the AI sparkle button to let Google Gemini automatically fill in the description, dates, and location.
5. **Save**: The event is now created in **Pipeline** status!
6. **AI Marketing Plan**: Back in the portfolio, click the Sparkles icon on your new event card to instantly generate a tailored event marketing plan powered by OpenClaw.

---

## 2. Add Target Companies & Attendees

Before scheduling, populate your directory. Attendees are linked to global **Companies**.

1. **Add Companies (Optional but recommended)**: Navigate to **Audience → Companies**. Create companies and assign an estimated **Pipeline Value ($)**. This is a crucial step for accurate ROI measurement.
2. **Add Attendees**: Navigate to **Audience → Attendees** and click **Add Attendee**.
3. **Data Required**:
    - **Name & Company**: Required. *Tip: Enter just these two, then click the **AI Auto Complete** button to fetch their Title, Bio, and LinkedIn URL automatically.*
    - **Email**: Required for sending calendar invites.
    - **Seniority Level**: E.g., C-Level, VP. *(Crucial for ROI KPIs).*

---

## 3. Set Up ROI Targets

*Access Level Required: Root or Marketing to submit/approve.*

Define what success looks like before the event starts.

1. **Navigate to ROI**: Go to **Performance → ROI**.
2. **Set Targets**: Under the **Targets & Approval** tab, enter your goals:
    - **Target Budget ($)**: The total allocated budget for this event.
    - **Expected Pipeline**: The total deal value you aim to generate.
    - **Win Rate**: Your expected conversion rate (e.g., 0.15 for 15%).
    - **Meeting KPIs**: Target number of meetings (e.g., Booth or C-Level meetings).
    - **Target Companies**: Select specific companies you *must* meet from the dropdown.
3. **Submit for Approval**: Once configured, click **Submit for Approval**. (A Root user must click **Approve** to lock these targets).

---

## 4. Schedule & Manage the Event

With your attendees and targets set, you can begin executing your event strategy.

1. **Create Rooms**: Go to **Logistics → Rooms** to define your meeting spaces and their capacities.
2. **Schedule & Manage Meetings**:
    - Go to **Logistics → Calendar** for a drag-and-drop view, or **Logistics → New Meeting** to use the full scheduling form.
    - **View Details**: Click any existing meeting to open a quick, read-only details modal. To make changes, click the **Edit** button within the modal.
    - Select your Attendees, Room, Date/Time, and set the Status to **Confirmed**.
3. **Monitor Progress**:
    - **Dashboard**: Go to **Performance → Dashboard** for a real-time overview of meeting counts and room utilization.
    - **ROI Tracker**: Go to **Performance → ROI** and check the **Performance Tracker** tab. As meetings occur, your Actual Pipeline, C-Level meetings, and Target Companies Hit will update **automatically** based on the scheduled meetings!
4. **Post-Event**: Once the event concludes, go to the **Event Execution** tab on the ROI page to record manual engagements (like Keynotes or Social Reach), and update the Event Status in **Settings** to **Occurred** to lock the data.
